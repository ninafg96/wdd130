Santa Fe, Argentina

Santa Fe it is ubicated in the Pampa region, and it is bordered by Chaco at the north, Corrientes and Entre Ríos at the east, Buenos Aires at the south, and Santiago del Estero and Córdoba at the west.
It is an agricultural and livestock province. Along its eastern border flows the Paraná River, one of the main waterways in South America.
The terrain is flat, and it has a very wet climate.
